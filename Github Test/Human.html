Human

Traits

Ability score increase: Either +1 to 2 stats of your choice and +2 to another, or +1 to all stats.
Age: Humans reach maturity about from ages 16-22, and can live, in optimal conditions, up to 100 years old.
Size: Medium
Speed: 30 feet.
Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common along with a number of languages equal to your intelligence modifier, none of which can be exotic.
Subrace: Humans, although diverse in culture, have very similar body plans among themselves compared to other races. Therefore, their race is generally uniform.

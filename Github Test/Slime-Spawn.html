Slime-Spawn

Traits

Speed: You gain a swimming speed of 30 feet.
Amphibious: You can breathe both air and water.
Disseminate: As an action, while underwater, you may effectively become one with the water, and travel along with it. Your equipment transforms with you. While in this form, you have the following traits:
    Size: Large.
    Speed: 15 feet swim speed.
    One with water: You are considered invisible, and incorporeal.
You may change out of this form as an action.

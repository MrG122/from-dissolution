Tar-Spawn

Traits

Seepage: You gain a burrowing speed of 5 feet.
Sticky Tar: As an action, you may attempt to stick an opponent to the ground. Make a touch attack against an opponent. If it hits, they must make a dexterity saving throw with a DC equal to 10 + your dexterity modifier. Upon a success, nothing happens. Upon a failure, the opponent is grappled to the ground and must make a strength check as an action to break out of it, equal to 10 + your constitution modifier.

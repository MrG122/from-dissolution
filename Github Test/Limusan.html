Limusan

Traits

Ability score increase: -1 to Charisma, +1 to Constitution.
Age: Limusans are at full maturity upon birth, and can live up to 20 years.
Size: Medium
Speed: 30 Feet
Regrow: You can use an action to gain a number of hit points equal to your level multiplied by 2. You cannot use this ability again until you finish a long rest.
Amourphous Form: You gain resistance to bludgeoning damage.
Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common along with a number of languages equal to your intelligence modifier. Exotic languages you may choose are: Aquan, Infernal, Ooze.
Subrace: Limusans are the spawn of Ooblex, the infernal ruler over the plane of Pandemonium. It created these spawn to spread chaos and destroy the planes, so that they may all be absorbed by its greatness. It created 3 varieties of Limusian; The Tar-Spawn, who focused mainly on ground-based altercations; The Slime-Spawn, which focused mainly on water-based altercations; and the Grey-Spawn, who focused on infiltration.
